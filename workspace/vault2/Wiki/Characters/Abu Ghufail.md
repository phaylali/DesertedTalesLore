# Abu Ghufair

## Core Identity

- **Name**: Abu Ghufair (Muhammad)
- **Title**: 4th Ruler of Barghawata Confederacy
- **Reign**: 888–913 CE (25 years)
- **Ethnicity**: Masmouda Berber
- **Symbol**: 🏰 (Consolidation)

## Key Details

- **Succession**: Took throne after Yunus's death
- **Religious Policy**: Maintained Barghawata doctrine but added personal interpretations
- **Marriage**: Had 44 wives
- **Reign Length**: 25 years (29th year of reign when died)

## Historical Significance

> "Consolidated Barghawata power after Yunus's revolutionary reign. His reign marked the transition from revolutionary state to established confederacy."

## Legacy

- **Military**: Continued 3,200 knight and 10,000 horsemen force
- **Diplomacy**: Relations with Umayyads remained complex but stable
- **Cultural**: Preserved unique religious practices while maintaining Berber identity
