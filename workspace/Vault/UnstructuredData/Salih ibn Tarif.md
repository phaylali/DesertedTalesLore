# Salih ibn Tarif

## Core Identity
- **Name**: Salih ibn Tarif
- **Title**: Founder of the Barghawata Confederacy
- **Ethnicity**: Masmouda Berber
- **Lifespan**: 710–791 CE
- **Reign**: 744–791 CE (47 years)
- **Symbol**: 🌊 (Atlantic coast, Tamesna region)

## Key Historical Context
- **Founding**: Established the Barghawata Confederacy in 744 CE in the Tamesna region (modern Casablanca area)
- **Tribal Base**: Initially 29 Masmouda tribes (12 adopted Barghawata religion, 17 remained orthodox)
- **Religious Strategy**: Practiced *taqiyyah* (religious concealment) - never publicly claimed prophethood during his lifetime

## The Great Mystery
| Fact | What Was Believed | What Actually Happened |
|------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Prophethood** | Claimed to be prophet (80-sura Koran in Tamazight) | Never publicly claimed this; only revealed to inner circle |
| **Disappearance** | Died at age 47 (791 CE) | Vanished mysteriously at kingdom's age 47; told son Ilyas "I will return" |
| **Prophecy** | Promised return during the 7th king's reign | The "return prophecy" was invented by Yunus 50+ years later |

## Legacy
- **Official Narrative**: "Prophet who received revelation in Tamazight; promised to return as Mahdi"
- **Historical Reality**: Sufri Kharijite leader who practiced religious concealment (*taqiyyah*)
- **Scholarly Note**: Talbi confirms "no contemporary record of him being anything other than a Sufri Kharijite" - prophetic claims likely propagated by Yunus

## The Plot Twist
> "Salih never claimed prophethood publicly. His grandson Yunus invented the entire religious system in 842 and attributed it to his grandfather to legitimize his political campaign."

## Religious Doctrine (Attributed)
- **God's Name**: Yakûsh (Berber for "the giver")
- **Holy Book**: 80 suras in Tamazight
- **Mahdi Claim**: Salih ibn Tarif (awaited return)
- **Unique Practices**: 
  - 10 daily prayers (5 day + 5 night)
  - Fasting during Rajab (not Ramadan)
  - Thursday assembly day
  - Half standing, half sitting prayer posture
  - Dietary taboos (eggs, rooster meat)
  - Allowed wild boar meat

## Timeline
- **744**: Founded Barghawata Confederacy
- **744–791**: Reign with religious concealment (*taqiyyah*)
- **791**: Disappeared mysteriously; told son Ilyas "I will return"
- **842**: Yunus revealed religion (50+ years later)
- **1058**: Confederacy destroyed by Almoravids
