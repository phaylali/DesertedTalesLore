# Younes Ibn Ulyas (Yunus Ibn Ilyas)

## Core Identity
- **Name**: Yunus ibn Ilyas
- **Title**: 3rd Ruler of Barghawata Confederacy
- **Reign**: 842–888 CE (46 years)
- **Ethnicity**: Masmouda Berber
- **Symbol**: 🔥 (Revolution)

## Key Details
- **Religious Revolution**: Publicly revealed Barghawata religion in 842 (50+ years after founding)
- **Prophecy**: Claimed grandfather Salih was a prophet
- **Political Strategy**: Killed 7,770 opponents to spread faith
- **Hajj**: Performed Hajj to maintain Islamic credentials

## Historical Significance
> "Yunus Ibn Ilyas created the Barghawata religion as we know it. By attributing it to his grandfather Salih, he legitimized Berber independence from Arab Islam."

## The Plot Twist
> "He invented the entire religion and retroactively attributed it to his grandfather to create political legitimacy. The '7th king prophecy' was a political tool to sustain the dynasty."

## Legacy
- **Religious**: Established 80-sura Tamazight Koran; made it official state religion
- **Military**: Expanded kingdom to control Atlantic plains
- **Cultural**: Created unique Berber religious identity
