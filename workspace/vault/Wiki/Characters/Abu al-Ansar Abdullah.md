# Abu al-Ansar Abdullah

## Core Identity
- **Name**: Abu al-Ansar Abdullah
- **Title**: 5th Ruler of Barghawata Confederacy
- **Reign**: 917–961 CE (44 years)
- **Ethnicity**: Masmouda Berber
- **Symbol**: 🌊 (Enduring)

## Key Details
- **Burial**: Died and was buried at Ameslakht
- **Diplomacy**: Maintained relations with Umayyads of Córdoba
- **Religious Policy**: Upheld Barghawata doctrine without major changes
- **Succession**: Passed throne to son Abu Mansur Isa

## Historical Significance
> "Preserved Barghawata confederacy during a period of relative stability, maintaining the religious identity established by his predecessors."

## Legacy
- **Cultural**: Continued unique religious practices
- **Diplomacy**: Strengthened ties with Umayyads of Córdoba
- **Economic**: Maintained trade networks across Atlantic plains
