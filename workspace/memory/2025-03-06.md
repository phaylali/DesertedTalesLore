## Research Completed

### Salih ibn Tarif (صالح بن طريف)
- **Source:** Arabic Wikipedia
- **Key Findings:**
  - Born ~728 CE (110 AH), died 744 CE (127 AH)
  - Founder of the Barghwata dynasty in Tamesna region (western Morocco)
  - Self-declared prophet in 744 CE, took title "صالح المؤمنين" (Salih the Believer)
  - Claimed to receive divine scripture (Kitab) in Berber language
  - Followers believed he was the awaited Mahdi and would return
  - Unique religious-political movement combining Islam with Berber traditions
  - Had claimed Jewish ancestry through his father Tarif
