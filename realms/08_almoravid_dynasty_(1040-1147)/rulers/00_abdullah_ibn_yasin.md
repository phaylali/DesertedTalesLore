# Abdullah ibn Yasin

## Reign

Abdullah ibn Yasin was the founder and spiritual leader of the Almoravid movement. He was a theologian of the Maliki school of Sunni Islam. His leadership was instrumental in uniting the Sanhaja Berber tribes and laying the foundation for the Almoravid empire. He died in battle in 1059.
