# Jurthum ibn Ahmad

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Jurthum ibn Ahmad ibn Ziyadat Allah
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely mid-10th century)
*   **Death:** 971
*   **Place of Birth:** Málaga (Andalus)
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Jurthum ibn Ahmad ruled Nekor from 948 to 971 CE, a 25-year reign marked by reconstruction and prosperity. Invited from Málaga in al-Andalus, he rebuilt the city devastated by invasions and restored it under Maliki Sunni orthodoxy.

## Skills and Abilities
Jurthum excelled in administration and urban renewal, transforming Nekor into a stable and thriving center.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** The restorer of Nekor, bringing peace and rebuilding after chaos.
*   **Associated Quests:** Quests involving reconstruction, economic revival, or religious reforms.
