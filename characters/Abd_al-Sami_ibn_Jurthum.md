# Abd al-Sami ibn Jurthum

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Abd al-Sami ibn Jurthum
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely mid-10th century)
*   **Death:** 948
*   **Place of Birth:** Nekor
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Abd al-Sami ibn Jurthum served as a nominal emir of Nekor from 941 to 948 CE, installed by the Miknasa leader Musa ibn Abi al-Afiya under Umayyad authority. His rule ended when he was killed in a popular revolt after Musa's death.

## Skills and Abilities
Abd al-Sami navigated the complexities of vassalage to larger powers, maintaining a semblance of local authority.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** Represents the erosion of Nekor's independence under external control.
*   **Associated Quests:** Quests about vassalage, revolts against overlords, or the loss of autonomy.
