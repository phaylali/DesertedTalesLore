## Biography
Al-Mu'tasim ibn Salih succeeded his father, Salih I ibn Mansur, as emir of Nekor in 749 CE. He was known for his exceptional piety, which distinguished him during his reign.

## Skills and Abilities
Al-Mu'tasim was renowned for his deep religious devotion and commitment to Islamic principles, serving as a moral exemplar for his subjects.
