# Idris I ibn Salih

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Idris I ibn Salih
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely mid-8th century)
*   **Death:** 760
*   **Place of Birth:** Nekor
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Idris I ibn Salih ruled Nekor from 749 to 760 CE. He is credited with founding the city of Nekor, which became the capital of the kingdom, establishing a central hub for governance and trade.

## Skills and Abilities
Idris demonstrated visionary leadership in urban development, transforming Nekor into a fortified and prosperous center.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** The architect of Nekor's capital, symbolizing growth and stability.
*   **Associated Quests:** Quests involving city-building, defense of the capital, or urban expansion.
