# Abd al-Badi ibn Salih

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Abd al-Badi ibn Salih
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely early 10th century)
*   **Death:** 935
*   **Place of Birth:** Nekor
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Abd al-Badi ibn Salih, also known as al-Mu'ayyad, ruled Nekor from approximately 927 to 929 CE. His reign ended tragically when he was defeated and killed by Musa ibn Abi'l-Afiya, leader of the Miknasa tribe, in 935 CE during a Fatimid-backed invasion.

## Skills and Abilities
Abd al-Badi was a courageous leader who fought valiantly to defend Nekor against superior forces.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** A tragic hero who perished defending Nekor's independence.
*   **Associated Quests:** Quests involving last stands, betrayals, or the fall of Nekor to invaders.
