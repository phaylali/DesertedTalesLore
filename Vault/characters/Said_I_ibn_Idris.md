# Said I ibn Idris

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Said I ibn Idris
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely late 8th century)
*   **Death:** 864
*   **Place of Birth:** Nekor
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Said I ibn Idris ruled Nekor from 803 to 864 CE, one of the longest reigns in the kingdom's history. His rule was marked by stability and resilience, including successfully defending against a Viking incursion in 859 CE, which threatened the coastal regions.

## Skills and Abilities
Said was a steadfast leader with strong military acumen, particularly in coastal defense against external threats like Viking raids.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** The enduring defender of Nekor, embodying courage against unexpected invaders.
*   **Associated Quests:** Quests involving naval defense, repelling raiders, or maintaining long-term stability.
