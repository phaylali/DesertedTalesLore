# Salih III ibn Sa'id

## Profile
*   **Full Name:** Salih III ibn Sa'id
*   **Birth:** Unknown (likely late 9th century)
*   **Death:** 927
*   **Place of Birth:** Nekor
*   **Tribal Origin:** Banu Salih
*   **Faction/Allegiance:** Kingdom of Nekor
*   **Role:** Emir

## Biography
Salih III ibn Sa'id ruled Nekor from 917 to 927 CE, a turbulent period marked by Fatimid attacks. Nekor was sacked by Fatimid forces in 917 and again in 934, testing the emirate's resilience.

## Skills and Abilities
Salih demonstrated resilience in leadership, defending Nekor against powerful external invasions.

## Game-Specific Information
*   **Role in the Story:** A warrior emir facing overwhelming odds from the Fatimid Caliphate.
