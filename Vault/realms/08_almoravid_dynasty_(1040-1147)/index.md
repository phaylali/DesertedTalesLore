# Almoravid Dynasty

## Summary

The Almoravid dynasty was a Berber imperial dynasty of Morocco, which formed an empire that stretched over the western Maghreb and al-Andalus. Founded by Abdullah ibn Yasin, their capital was Marrakech, and they ruled from 1040 to 1147.
