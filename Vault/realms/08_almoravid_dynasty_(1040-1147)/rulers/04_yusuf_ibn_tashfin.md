# Yusuf ibn Tashfin

## Reign

Yusuf ibn Tashfin is considered the most prominent ruler of the Almoravid dynasty. He was the co-founder of Marrakech and led the Almoravid conquest of al-Andalus, famously defeating the Castilians at the Battle of Sagrajas.
