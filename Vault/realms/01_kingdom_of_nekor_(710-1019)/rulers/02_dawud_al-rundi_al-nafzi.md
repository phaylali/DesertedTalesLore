# Dawud al-Rundi al-Nafzi

## Reign

Dawud al-Rundi al-Nafzi was chosen by the Berber tribes of Nekor to replace Salih I ibn Mansur in 712 CE, after they grew tired of the restrictions of Islam. His rule was short-lived, as the tribes were not satisfied with his leadership and reinstated Salih I.
